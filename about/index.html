I am the Next Search Catalog Coordinator at Northeast Kansas Library System in Lawrence, KS, which is also known as NEKLS.

117 NEKLS Libraries in the highlighted area.

NEKLS provides many services to all types of libraries in north east Kansas.

The 14 highlighted counties account for approximately 44% of the population of Kansas.

A lot of people in the Koha community think NEKLS=the shared catalog we operate here. But NEKLS is actually a regional library system as established in the late 60s by Kansas law and we have 117 member libraries in the 14 counties in the upper right hand corner of the state where Kansas, Nebraska, and Missouri all meet along the Missouri River.
